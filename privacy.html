FouFou — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

FouFou ("the App") is a city trail generator for Bangkok and Singapore, developed by Eitan Fisher. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

Anonymous users (no account)

  • No personal data is collected.
  • A temporary anonymous session ID is created locally to enable basic app functionality.
  • We collect aggregated, non-identifiable access statistics (country of access, weekly visit count). No IP addresses are stored.

Registered users (optional account)

  • Email address and display name — provided during sign-up via Google or email.
  • Places you contribute — location name, coordinates, and description, stored in our shared database for all users to benefit from.
  • Reviews and ratings — associated with your user ID.
  • User preferences — interests and settings stored to personalize your experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate personalized city trails based on your interests.
  • To display places, reviews, and ratings contributed by the community.
  • To improve the app using aggregated (anonymous) usage statistics.
  • We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and database. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Places API — place names, ratings, and photos from Google Maps. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps — for navigation directions.

4. Location Data

The App may request your device location to find trails near you. Location data is used only within the app session and is never stored on our servers without your explicit action (e.g. contributing a place).

5. Data Retention

  • Anonymous sessions: not stored.
  • Registered accounts: retained until you request deletion.
  • Contributed places and reviews: retained as community content. You may request removal by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children

FouFou is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced within the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
